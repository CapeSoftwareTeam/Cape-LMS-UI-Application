CAPE ELECTRIC PRIVATE LIMITED


Leave Policy for the Year 2022

Objective

Leave Policy aims at providing the employees an opportunity to take a planned break from the stress ofbusiness so as to re-energize him/her and achieve a better balance between work and another social familyresponsibilities.

The annual cycle is normally the calendar year –1st January to 31st December. The Employees, who are on probation,will be able to take one day of leave per month. All confirmed employees are entitled to leave,according to the rules and regulations stated below.

Casual Leave : 5 Days

Sick Leave : 5 Days

If the SL is taken more than 2 days Medical Certificate should be provided.

Privilege Leave : 15Days

All permanent employees are eligible for 15calendar day’s privilege leave. For employees joiningmidyear, leave will be computed from date of confirmation on pro-rata basis and would be creditedto the employees account on 1st January each year. Employee should apply for the leave before 3workingdays to their immediate Supervisor.

Maternity Leave : As per norms

Public Holidays : 12 Days

All Employees are advised to take proper approval in case of an absence of 3 Consecutive days. Absence without proper approval will be considered for Loss Of Pay for any leave requirement.

Annually two times, Friday+Saturday+Sunday+Monday (2 Days Leave & 2 Days Holidays) will not be treated as 4 days leave. Prior Approval required from Sales Head / HR Department.

Working Hours : 8.30 AM to 5.30 PM

Working Days : Monday to Saturday except 2 and 4th Saturday.

Lunch Time : 1:30 PM to 2:00 PM
